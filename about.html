About Me

I am in my senior year as a technical communication undergraduate at Iowa State University. My wife, Sarah, and I live with our four children in a small town in Central Iowa. I am particularly interested in website design and other multi-modal forms of communication.


Education

My diverse interests are well demonstrated by my equally diverse educational background. In addition to a B.A. in technical communication, I hold a B.S. in economics, a B.A. in political science, and certification as a Naval Nuclear Power Plant operator.


Military Service

I proudly served in the U.S. Navy as an electrician and nuclear power plant operator from 1999 to 2005. I deployed with the U.S.S. Theodore Roosevelt on September 19th, 2001 in support of Operation Enduring Freedom. I was attached to U.S.S. Shreveport during her deployment in 2004 in support of Operation Iraqi Freedom. During my service, I earned two personal commendations from my commanding officers and the Naval Achievement Medal.


Early ACCESS Iowa

In November 2018, I was appointed by Iowa Gov. Kim Reynolds to the Iowa Council for Early ACCESS. Early ACCESS provides early intervention services for children from birth to three years who experience developmental delays or who have a documented disability likely to cause developmental delays. In 2019, I was elected to the position of Vice Chair by my fellow board members.
